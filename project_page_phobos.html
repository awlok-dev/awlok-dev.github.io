Phobos Oddity Hong Kong

About the Game

Platform: PC

"Phobos Oddity - Hong Kong" is an indie game that brings together the rich culture of Hong Kong and elements of horror. Through meticulously crafted visuals and atmosphere, you will traverse the dim corners of public housing estates, explore mysterious temples, confront the conspiracies of cults, and even invaders. Each chapter is a unique psychological challenge, waiting for you to unravel..
