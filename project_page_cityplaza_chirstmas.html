City Plaza Christmas 2024

About the Project

Platform: PC

A festive Christmas event created for City Plaza, a premier shopping mall in Hong Kong. The interactive installation featured three distinct games: a dancing game, a rhythm game, and a digital graffiti experience. Visitors could engage with colorful interfaces to express themselves through movement, test their musical timing, and create virtual street art, all while enjoying a unique blend of technology and holiday cheer throughout the shopping experience.
