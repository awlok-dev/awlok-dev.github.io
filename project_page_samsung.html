Samsung Showroom Interactive Motion Game

About the Game

Platform: PC

An interactive motion-sensing experience developed for Samsung's showroom displays. This project uses Azure Kinect sensor to detect visitors' movements and gestures, instantly translating them into captivating visual effects on Samsung screens. Players can wave their hands to control particle systems, strike poses to trigger animations, and create dynamic content that showcases the responsiveness and visual capabilities of Samsung displays. Perfect for demonstrating the high refresh rates and color accuracy of Samsung products while creating a memorable, engaging experience for showroom visitors.
